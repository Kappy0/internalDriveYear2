Every Smash Bros. game, while keeping the core gameplay intact, have different rules due to the unique physics present in each game. Here are the more standard rules for every game so far in the series.

NOTE: Stagelists are different depending on where the tournament is held and who is running the tournament. The stages I listed are the ones the majority of the community uses at every tournament.

Super Smash Bros. 64

  • - 5 stock
  • - 8 Minute Timer
  • - Items Off
  • - Best of 3
  • Stages
    • - Dreamland
    • - Hyrule Castle

Super Smash Bros. Melee

  • - 4 stock
  • - 8 Minute Timer
  • - Items Off
  • - Best of 3
  • Stages
    • - Final Destination
    • - Battlefield
    • - Dreamland
    • - Yoshi's Story
    • - Pokemon Stadium

Super Smash Bros. Brawl

  • - 3 stock
  • - 8 Minute Timer
  • - Items Off
  • - Best of 3
  • Stages
    • - Final Destination
    • - Battlefield
    • - Smashville
    • - Yoshi's Island
    • - Pokemon Stadium 1
    • - Lylat Cruise
    • - Frigate Orpheon
