Ryan Klaproth!

Ryan "Kappy" "SwagTrain" Klaproth has been playing Super Smash Bros. since Super Smash Bros. 64 first hit store shelves. It wasn't until Super Smash Bros. Brawl was released that he decided to take part in the competitive community, and he's been loving it ever since. Since then, he's taken up competitive Super Smash Bros. Melee and quit Super Smash Bros. Brawl. Some of his Smash Bros. accomplishments are being one of the top 10 players in Illinois and one of the top 5 Kirby players in the country in Super Smash Bros. Brawl.

Hobbies and School

Some of Kappy's hobbies include dancing, bike riding, and playing competitive Pokemon. He is currently a 5th year student at DePaul University with a major in Computer Games Development (focus in programming) and a minor in Computer Science.
